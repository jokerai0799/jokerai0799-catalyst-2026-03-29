Quote follow up software

What Quote Chaser is built to do

Many small trades and service businesses send solid estimates but lose work because follow up happens too late, gets forgotten, or lives in too many places. Quote Chaser gives you a simple quote follow up workflow so each estimate stays visible after it has been sent.

Instead of relying on memory, inbox searches, or spreadsheets, teams can track sent quotes, see which jobs need a reminder, and keep ownership clear across the pipeline. That makes Quote Chaser useful as quote chasing software for teams that want a focused workflow rather than a full CRM.

For businesses that send estimates every week, this also works as estimate follow up software: each quote can carry a value, status, owner, sent date, next follow up date, and note history, so the whole team can see what needs action next.

Use cases

Ways teams use Quote Chaser to stay on top of follow ups

Quote Chaser helps different kinds of trades and service businesses keep sent quotes visible, organised, and easier to follow up.

Quote chasing software

If someone is looking for quote chasing software, they usually want a faster way to see who needs a callback, email, or reminder today. Quote Chaser keeps that queue visible so follow ups stop slipping through the cracks.

Estimate follow up software

Estimate follow up software should help teams move from "quote sent" to "next action due" without manual admin. Quote Chaser is structured around that handoff, so you can manage estimates after they leave your inbox.

Quote reminders software for trades

Trades businesses often need simple quote reminders software rather than complex sales software. Quote Chaser is aimed at that practical use case: clear reminders, clear ownership, and a simple today view.

Track sent quotes

Businesses searching for ways to track sent quotes usually want one place to see status, value, and follow up dates. Quote Chaser keeps those records visible and easier to act on.
