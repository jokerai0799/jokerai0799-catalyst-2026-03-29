Refund Policy

Quote Chaser

Refunds and cancellations

This Refund Policy explains how cancellations, billing issues, and refund requests are handled for Quote Chaser.

Trials

If a free trial is offered, you can use it to evaluate the service before moving to a paid plan.

Subscription charges

Subscriptions are generally billed in advance for the agreed period. Unless we say otherwise, charges that have already been processed are normally non-refundable, except where required by law.

Cancelling a plan

You can cancel future renewals before the next billing date. Cancelling usually stops future charges, but it does not normally create a refund for the current billing period.

Refund requests

If you believe there has been a billing error, duplicate payment, or a serious service problem, contact us with the relevant details and we will review the request fairly.

Exceptional refunds

Where we choose to issue a refund, it will usually be returned to the original payment method where possible. Timing may depend on the payment provider or bank.

Contact

For refund or billing questions, contact quotefollowup@outlook.com.
