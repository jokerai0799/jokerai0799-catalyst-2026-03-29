Estimate follow up software

Why teams look for estimate follow up software

A lot of teams send estimates and then rely on memory, inboxes, or scraps of paper to decide what to do next. That usually means good opportunities drift because nobody is quite sure which estimate needs a call, reminder, or status update.

Quote Chaser gives you a simpler estimate follow up workflow: each estimate can stay visible with its value, current status, next follow up date, and owner, so the team can see what should happen next instead of guessing.

That makes it useful for businesses that want estimate follow up software rather than a full CRM or sales system. The goal is simple: keep sent estimates moving until they are won, lost, or clearly closed out.

Use cases

How teams use it

Most teams need estimate follow up software for a few practical jobs. These are the ones Quote Chaser is built around.

Track sent estimates

Keep estimate value, sent date, status, and next action visible in one place so your team is not searching messages or spreadsheets.

See what needs chasing next

Estimate follow up works best when the next step is obvious. Quote Chaser helps teams see which estimates are due today or overdue.

Keep ownership clear

When more than one person is involved, follow up often stalls because nobody knows who owns it. A clear owner helps the process keep moving.

Stay focused without CRM bloat

If the real need is estimate tracking and follow up, a simpler workflow is often more useful than a heavy CRM rollout.
