Quote management software for trades

Why trade businesses look for this

Trades and service businesses often need quote management software because the real challenge starts after the quote has been sent. The team needs to know what stage each quote is in, what happens next, and which jobs are still active.

Quote Chaser keeps that workflow simpler. Instead of managing quotes through a general-purpose CRM, teams can track sent dates, current status, next follow ups, and won or lost outcomes in one focused place.

That makes it a better fit for businesses that want practical quote management for trades: clear pipeline visibility, cleaner follow up, and less clutter around the core job.
