Quote management software for trades

Keep quote status, next actions, and outcomes in one place

Quote Chaser gives trade and service businesses one clear place to track sent quotes, current status, next follow up dates, and outcomes without the extra CRM clutter.

It is built for the part that usually gets messy after the quote goes out: what stage each job is in, what should happen next, and what has already been won or closed out.

  • Track quote status clearly — sent, follow-up due, won, and lost quotes stay visible.
  • Keep the pipeline readable — the team can see what is still active and what needs action next.
  • Work without CRM bloat — the workflow stays focused on quotes and follow ups.
